CardSharp- Intro

CardSharp is a matching game where you have to match icons. The game is played with a deck of 10 pairs of picture/icons cards. The game displays the cards face down in a 5x4 array in randomised positions. You have to click on one square, the picture/icon shows, then another square, and it too shows its secrets. Once you match the icons they stay on the grid. Once all matched it will show a message and then closes the game. Here is a step by step guide to play the game:
